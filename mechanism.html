FOUR BAR LINKAGE

Design of a four bar linkage such that a point on the linkage coupler will go through three points on an attached sheet in order.
The dynamics of linkages can be used for constrained motion or several other applications to convert rotational motion to reciprocating motion and vice-versa or. Some of the uses include pumpjack for oil drilling, internal combustion engines, loaders and many more.
