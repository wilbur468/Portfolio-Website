Bit More About Me

Born and raised on the foothills of the Himalays, I am originally from Nepal. As I grew up in a country rich with different cultures and traditions, I quickly became acclimated to the diverse environment within the U.S. However, my accent still remains distinct. I am currently a sophomore at the University of New Orleans located in 'The Cresent City'.

I have always had a keen interset towards engineering and computers. Like the cliched story of many engineers, I would poke things around to see how they worked. My interset in computers, however, arised becuase I would spend hours seeing others play games in Internet Cafes (Yes, computers were a luxury).

I like to keep myself busy in different activities. I am currently involved in different engineering and computer science societies. I am also a huge sports lover. I played professional archery for about 4 years, and managed to grab some medals in National Chamionships. I enjoy watching and playing soccer (or shall I say football :), basketball and volleyball. A normal day in my life includes juggling variety of activities and finally lying down in my beloved bed.
